Case study

Setup:

  • Blink XT Camera

  • Cox network - Oklahoma

  • Netgear R6300 V2 Wireless AP (operating in 2.4 GHz mode)

  • Kali Linux 2020.1

  • Aircrack-ng 1.6

The Blink XT2 Camera is deployed on the wall 1.6 m above the ground.

We use the Aircrack-ng tool to lock on the MAC address of the wireless camera and then monitor the traffic associated with the MAC.
